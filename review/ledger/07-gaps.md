# Claim Ledger — Module 07 (`learning/07-repo-links-and-gaps.md`)

Audited against `learning/review/ground-truth/` (peat HEAD `35d0f11`, peat-mesh `00ab0c9`/rc.42,
peat-btle `3d70f48`/0.4.0, peat-lite `7a8a8fb`/0.2.5, peat-gateway `8d16824`/0.1.0,
peat-node `4e1b5c8`/0.4.3). Verdicts: Verified · Outdated · Misleading · Oversimplified · Wrong · Unverifiable · Speculative-unlabeled.

This module is a repo-map/links page, so most claims are structural (what exists, where) rather
than protocol claims. The high-risk transport/CRDT/FIPS/NodeId/hierarchy items are largely *out of
scope* for this module's text — it makes almost none of them. The most material problems are
(1) treating the real, substantial `peat-node` repo as an uncertain "maybe a repackaging,"
(2) a stale ADR count, and (3) a couple of ADR-number→title mismatches and missing shipped/proposed
labels on referenced components.
