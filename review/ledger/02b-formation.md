## Summary of high-risk findings

1. **WRONG — leaf hierarchy tier named `Platform`** (§2·5.1) but peat-mesh source ships `Node = 0` (`beacon/types.rs:56-67`). The rename has not landed in peat-mesh; ADR-068 (`Platform→Node`) is open churn. Most embarrassing catchable error.
2. **WRONG — `CellRole` includes a fabricated `Support` variant** (§2·5.6). Real enum is 6 variants `Leader/Sensor/Compute/Relay/Strike/Follower` (`models/role.rs:14-24`). `Support` does not exist.
3. **MISLEADING — ADR-066 presented as "authoritative, current"** without a Proposed label; the entire module leans on Proposed ADRs (066, 060, 024?) as if ratified.
4. **OVERSIMPLIFIED/SPECULATIVE — the per-field CRDT-type table** (members=OR-Set, leader_id=LWW-Register, capabilities=G-Set) presents a teaching abstraction as the implemented mechanism; CellState is an **Automerge** document, and typed OR-Set/LWW/G-Set primitives live in the embedded leaf crates (peat-lite/peat-btle), not in the CellState path. `OrSet` has no struct impl even there.
5. **UNVERIFIABLE quantitative claims** — leadership-score weights (0.30/0.25/0.20/0.15/0.10), all formation/election timing defaults (5s/2s/3-missed/0.7 readiness/min-size-3), and the message-round election machinery. The one scoring function ground truth documents (peat-mesh) uses *different* inputs and a vote-free beacon-observed model, contradicting the doc's round-based `LeaderElectionManager`/`CellMessageBus` state machine. These need direct `peat-protocol/src/cell/` verification; several may be fabricated.
6. **Vocabulary-drift risk in §2·5.10** — `CohortSummary` etc. may be `SquadSummary` in the still-legacy peat-schema proto (`squad_id` present).
